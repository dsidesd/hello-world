Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship cakes?
Unfortunately, we do not ship cakes. However, we are able to ship cake toppers.

I don't live near Austin, Texas. Can Natalie Sideserf come to my location to provide a cake or do a demonstration at my event?
Yes, Natalie does travel. Contact us for more details.

How much do you charge for custom cakes?
We price our sculpted cakes based on the complexity of your specific design. Our wedding cakes start at $5.50 per serving with additional cost for decorations.

How do I transport my cake to my event location?
Custom cakes can be delicate and are temperature sensitive, so Sideserf Cake Studio provides delivery to approved locations.

How do I order a cake?
You can contact us by phone, email, or request a quote. Your cake is officially booked only when your completed Statement of Work is submitted and a non-refundable 50% deposit is received.

Where are you located?
We do not have a brick and mortar location at this time. Renting out kitchen space on a per cake basis allows us to travel to different locations to provide cakes, classes, and demonstrations to a larger audience.

Consultations are by appointment-only.
